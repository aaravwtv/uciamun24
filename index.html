Frequently Asked Questions

Below you can find our frequently asked questions where we have meticulously identified major questions regarding our MUN and how it works and various frequently asked questions, if you do have further questions please dont feel afraid to Contact Us.

What is MUN?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational activity that replicates the workings of the United Nations. Participants, typically students, assume the roles of delegates representing different countries or organizations. They engage in debates, negotiate, and draft resolutions on various global issues. MUN conferences provide a platform for students to enhance skills like public speaking, research, diplomacy, and problem-solving.

Is this MUN paid?

Yes, UCIAMUN is a paid conference where each individual delegation or a school delegation or a chair will have to pay to be a part of this MUN and gain the best experience.
